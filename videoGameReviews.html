Video Game Reviews

Video games play a major role in my life and so I've decided to share a few of my favorite games with you and tell you a little bit about them.

Name Developer Game play Game style Player versatility
Dark souls 3 FromSoftware Within the story you take place as a ashen one who has been brought back to life to put the primary bosses to death and soon rekindle the flame that keeps life from decay. Dark souls 3 places emphasis on dodging attacks rather then taking them head on and requires strategy to take on even basic enemies let alone the bosses. Dark souls 3 offers several starting classes and allows the leveling of different stats And collection of new weapons to diversify charaters.
Assassin's creed revelations Ubisoft You follow Ezio Auditore as he unlocks the mystery to the apple of Eden while fighting off the evil that seeks the apple for power, the Templars. Assassin's creed revelations follows a focus on stealth and assassination, but has a full frontal combat system as well. There is little to customization besides a few different weapon choices.
XCOM: Enemy Unknown Firaxis Games XCOM has you play as earth's last defence against invading aliens with far more advanced tech. XCOM plays as a turn based strategy game where every move and attack can mean the difference between victory or defeat. XCOM lets you take control of soldiers that have different class that have different skills and abilities for you to choose from, along with the option to fully remake the look of your soldiers.
