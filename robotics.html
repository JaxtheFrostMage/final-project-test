Robotics

How I discovered robotics

When I came to West High school I was a bit lost on where I felt I belonged, But I knew I had a passion for engineering. I had heard about the robotics club that West hosted and I took an interest to the idea and decided to come by for their first meeting. It was here that I learned of FIRST and FTC (FIRST Tech Challenge). Ever since then I have been a proud member of FTC team #5466 the Combustible Lemons, and plan to stay with them for the rest of my high school career.
